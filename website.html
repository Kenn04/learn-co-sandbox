How To Be A Teen

We suck, sorry...

Teenagers suck. Everyone knows it too, it's public information at this point. No one likes us.
But, it's not really our fault to be honest. Our brains seek more rewards and usually more thrill
seeking as well. This explains why bad decisions are a "go to" for us.We as humans don't even fully
develope a good sense of self control until our early 20's.
